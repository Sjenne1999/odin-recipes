B and L's Strawberry Smoothie

Description

These pancakes are requested regularly by my family for breakfast. The recipe is easy to throw together and the taste is yummy!

Ingredients

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • 1/2 cup skim milk
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk

    Step 1

  1. In a blender combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar and vanilla. Toss in the ice. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into glasses and serve.
